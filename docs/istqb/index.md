# ISTQB Certification

Section dedicated to the **ISTQB® Certified Tester Foundation Level (CTFL) v4.0**, the reference entry certification for professional testing. I got certified in December 2024, and here I collect what I wish I'd had organized while preparing for it.

- [The CTFL v4.0 exam](/istqb/exam-format) — format, chapters, scoring, K-levels and where to download the official documents.
- [Study plan](/istqb/study-plan) — how to prepare it combining the official material with the articles on this wiki.

::: warning About the official material
The syllabus, sample exams and glossary are ISTQB copyrighted material. This section does not reproduce them: it **links** to them (they're free to download from the official site) and explains the exam **in my own words**. Always study from the original source; this section helps you get organized and know what to expect.
:::
